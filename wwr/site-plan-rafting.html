Overview

Purpose

The purpose of establishing an online presence for our rafting business is to reach a wider audience of adventure enthusiasts who may be searching for thrilling outdoor experiences. By showcasing our offerings, safety measures, and customer testimonials online, we aim to inspire confidence and excitement in potential customers, ultimately driving bookings and expanding our business reach. Our online platform will serve as a convenient hub for booking trips, accessing information, and engaging with our community of adventure seekers.

Audience

1. Who are the target customers? Our target customers are adventure-seeking individuals aged 18 to 45, primarily residing in urban areas but yearning for outdoor excitement. They are financially stable enough to afford recreational activities and prioritize experiences over material possessions. These thrill-seekers crave adrenaline-pumping adventures and enjoy exploring nature in a safe and exhilarating manner. 2. What do they want? They want memorable experiences that offer an escape from their daily routines, allowing them to reconnect with nature and unleash their adventurous spirit. They seek opportunities to bond with friends and family while creating lasting memories through exciting outdoor activities like white-water rafting. 3. What are their needs that aren't being met? Their needs revolve around finding reputable and safe adventure providers that offer thrilling experiences without compromising on safety. They desire convenient booking processes, knowledgeable guides, top-notch equipment, and a seamless overall experience to ensure their adventure is memorable for all the right reasons.
